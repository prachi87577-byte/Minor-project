⚠️ Challenges

Farmer Crop Selling Problems

📉 Low Crop Prices Farmers receive unfair dealer rates, reducing profit. Key causes include market glut, middlemen exploitation, limited market access, weather unpredictability, and global price fluctuations.
🤝 Middlemen Issues Middlemen leverage information asymmetry to offer low prices. Solutions include FPOs, direct-to-consumer sales, digital platforms like eNAM, and improved institutional credit access.
🚛 Transport & Storage Poor logistics causes crop damage and delays. Cold storage capacity is unevenly distributed. Government subsidies and community storage initiatives can greatly improve access.
💳 Payment Delays Late payments affect farmer finances. Digital payment systems and mobile banking ensure faster, more reliable transactions and reduce dependence on cash-based intermediaries.
